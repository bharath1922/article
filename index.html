Abhishek Sharma

SRH young opener, Abhishek Sharma has carved his name in the IPL record books for scoring the highest ever individual score by an Indian in the history of the tournament. He has shown his talent both on the domestic and international front.

Bhuvi

Bhuvneshwar Kumar, aka Bhuvi, is also known as the swing king of cricket. Bhuvneshwar is one of the best SRH pacers with impressive wicket hauls including his best bowling figures of 5 wickets for 19 runs against Kings XI Punjab (now Punjab Kings) in 2017.

Ishan Kishan

Ishan Kishan said he picked up the phone quite soon after being bought by Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) for INR 11.25 crore (USD 1.34 million approx.) at the IPL 2025 mega auction. He wanted to know what it would be like to play for the IPL's most explosive batting side.

NKR

Indian Cricket Team all-rounder Nitish Kumar Reddy has lashed out at rumours of him leaving Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) to play for some other team in Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026. He said that he always stands by his team and will continue playing for them.
